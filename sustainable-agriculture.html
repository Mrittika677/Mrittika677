PROJECT: SUSTAINABLE FARMING
  • Agriculture
  • Location: Dattapukur, North 24 Parganas
  • Our Role: SCIENTIFIC ADVISOR, IOT TOOL

Challenge

 

Dattapukur's agricultural community faces a crisis due to low productivity, outdated techniques, poor soil quality, and lack of access to modern tools. Crop yields decline, and erratic weather and inadequate irrigation infrastructure exacerbate the situation. The lack of support for innovation and market linkages isolates the farming community, making them vulnerable to poverty and food insecurity.

Solution

 

Our solution for this to advanced agricultural practices, such as multi-cropping, can optimize land use and income sources, boost productivity, enhance soil fertility, reduce pest risks, and improve climate resilience. Sustainable farming techniques, like organic inputs and climate-resilient crop varieties, strengthen agricultural systems, secure livelihoods, and transform agricultural productivity for a prosperous future.
